# Vammo - Data Analyst / Junior Data Scientist

**Role Description:**
We are looking for a Data Analyst to help us understand user behavior and optimize our business strategies. The ideal candidate will have strong SQL and Python skills, experience with A/B testing, and a passion for turning raw data into actionable business insights. Experience with predictive modeling (e.g., Churn prediction) is a huge plus.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Analyze large datasets to identify trends and patterns in user mobility and retention.
- Design and evaluate A/B tests to improve the user experience and increase retention.
- Build predictive models using Python (Scikit-learn, Pandas).
- Create dashboards in Metabase to communicate KPIs to stakeholders.

**Requirements:**
- Degree in Engineering, Computer Science, Statistics, or a related field.
- Proficiency in Python and SQL.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Ability to translate technical findings into business impact.
