# 🚀 Career and Evolution Roadmap

This roadmap is designed based on your current background (Mechanical/Materials Engineering, Data Analysis, and AI Agent Development). The focus is to accelerate your professional evolution, creating a hybrid and highly valued profile in the market: **An Engineer with strong analytical and programming skills**.

---

## 📊 1. Data Science & Analytics
Based on your interest in the Vammo role and Churn analysis:
- **Practical Statistics and A/B Testing:** Fundamental for analytical positions (as noted in the Vammo report: "should be tested other types of guarantee... followed by A/B testing").
- **BI Tools (Metabase / PowerBI):** You already have contact with Metabase instances. Deepen your skills in dashboard creation and data visualization.
- **Advanced SQL:** Window functions, CTEs (Common Table Expressions), and query optimization.
- **Machine Learning (Basic to Intermediate):** Consolidate your studies in `scikit-learn` and pandas (e.g., logistic regression models, random forest for churn prediction).

## 💻 2. Software Engineering & AI
You have already built a Finance Agent in Python using ChromaDB and Discord. To take the next step:
- **Software Engineering (Clean Code and Architecture):** Learn to structure larger projects. Study SOLID principles and Design Patterns.
- **Version Control & CI/CD:** Master Git/GitHub and learn to create automation pipelines (GitHub Actions) to test your bots.
- **AI Engineering & RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation):** Deepen your knowledge of vector databases (ChromaDB, Pinecone), LangChain, or LlamaIndex.
- **Docker & Cloud (AWS / GCP / Render):** Learn how to "containerize" your agents using Docker and deploy them to the cloud to run 24/7 independently of your local machine.

## ⚙️ 3. Product / Process Engineering
Leveraging your college studies and internship background (Vibration, Sealing, and Welding Tests):
- **Technical Documentation:** "Prepared technical documentation, including engineering specifications, reports, and test plans". Keep improving this skill; knowing how to communicate technical specs is rare.
- **Design of Experiments (DoE):** Learn how to structure physical validation tests (acrylic, aluminum) in a statistically valid way.
- **Internet of Things (IoT):** A great bridge between Physical Engineering and Software. Learn how to capture data from a vibration test using sensors and analyze it with Python.

## 🤝 4. Soft Skills & Management
- **Communicating Results:** Learn to translate technical data (churn rate, sealing results) into business impact ($, customer retention).
- **Time Management & Second Brain:** Keep this Obsidian vault organized using the PARA method (Projects, Areas, Resources, Archives) to avoid losing any learnings.
